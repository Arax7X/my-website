Features

Considered to the last pixel.

Aurelia isn't a theme with a nice screenshot. It's a complete, opinionated system — a live 3D cosmos, a token-driven design language, and the kind of finish you'd expect from a studio that charges by the week.

Design system

One language, every page.

Space Grotesk for display, Inter for reading, JetBrains Mono for the HUD-style labels, and a steel-to-cyan accent that runs cold and technical. Because every page shares the same stylesheet, your site is coherent by construction — not by luck.

Change a handful of tokens and the entire kit re-skins in seconds — buttons, glows, gradients, even the colours of the 3D globe. Colour, radius, shadow, and type are all controlled from one place.
