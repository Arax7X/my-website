Our story

A quiet obsession with the details.

Aurelia began with a frustration: the web is full of templates that look almost right. We wanted the ones that look exactly right — and we couldn't find them, so we made them.

Why we exist

Taste, made accessible.

Great design has always been a moat only a few could afford. A studio charges five figures and months of time; everyone else settles for something that undermines their work before a visitor reads a word.

We think that's backwards. So we distilled the way a top studio thinks — about type, rhythm, colour and restraint — into a kit anyone can pick up. You bring the story. Aurelia makes sure it's dressed for the room.
