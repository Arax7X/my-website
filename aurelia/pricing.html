Billing questions

Good to know.

Is the 14-day trial really free?+

Completely. Full access to all six pages, the 3D background, and both themes for fourteen days — no card up front. We remind you before it ends, and you only pick a plan if you decide to stay.

What happens to my site if I cancel?+

Anything you've already built stays live and yours forever — it's just static files on your host. You simply stop receiving new templates and updates from the day you cancel. No lock-in, no clawback.

Can I change tiers later?+

Any time, in either direction. Upgrades apply immediately and are prorated; downgrades take effect at your next billing date.

Why a subscription instead of buying once?+

Because Aurelia keeps growing — new pages, new templates, and refinements land every quarter, and you keep getting them. For agencies, the Studio tier pays for itself on a single client project.
